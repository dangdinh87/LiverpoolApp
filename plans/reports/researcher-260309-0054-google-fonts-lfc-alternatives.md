# Google Fonts Alternatives to LFC Brand Typeface

## Key Finding
Liverpool FC uses custom **LFC Sans** (modern brand) & **Albertus** (historic logo). Best Google Fonts matches below.

## Top Recommendations

### 1. **Anton** ⭐ (Best Overall)
- **Rationale**: Ultra-bold, ultra-condensed, heavy strokes = most aggressive sporty aesthetic matching LFC brand power
- **Vietnamese**: ✅ Full support
- **Weights**: 400 (single weight, naturally bold)
- **Status**: Already on your headline rotation (via Oswald); Anton is bolder alternative

### 2. **League Gothic** (Alternative)
- **Rationale**: Classic condensed all-caps display font; used in sports branding; strong industrial feel
- **Vietnamese**: ✅ Supported (via variable version Oct 2020)
- **Weights**: 400 only (no bold variant)
- **Caveat**: Single weight limits flexibility vs current setup

## Current Setup Assessment
- **Bebas Neue**: No Vietnamese support (open issue #8326) — replace if i18n required
- **Oswald**: Solid choice; middle ground between Anton aggression & League Gothic elegance
- **Barlow Condensed**: Good label font; 18 weights available; check Vietnamese support
