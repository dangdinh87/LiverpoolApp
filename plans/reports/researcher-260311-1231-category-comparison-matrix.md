## Implementation Hint: Transfer News vs. Rumours

If you decide to split `transfer` into separate categories:

**Transfer News** (confirmed/official)
- "Liverpool sign Mohamed Salah"
- "Salah signs new contract"
- "Player leaves on loan"

**Transfer Rumours** (speculation/unconfirmed)
- "Salah linked with move to MLS"
- "Liverpool interested in target"
- "Reports suggest club exploring options"

This distinction helps readers separate fact from speculation. Used by:
